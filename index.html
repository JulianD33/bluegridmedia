Frequently Asked Questions About LSA Management

How much does Google LSA management cost with Blue Grid Media?

Our pricing is simple and transparent: a one-time $300 setup fee to get your LSA account verified, optimized, and live, followed by $150/month for ongoing management. There are no long-term contracts — you can cancel anytime. See full pricing details.

What industries do you manage Local Services Ads for?

We specialize in 11 home service industries: HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Roofing, Pest Control, Landscaping, Carpet Cleaning, Tree Service, Appliance Repair, Water Damage Restoration, and Garage Door. Each industry has unique LSA considerations we've mastered through hundreds of campaigns. Read our industry-specific guides.

How long does it take to set up Google Local Services Ads?

Most accounts are verified and go live within 7 to 10 business days once you provide your documentation (license, insurance) and pass Google's background verification. Timelines can vary slightly by industry and region. Learn about the Google Guaranteed process.

What kind of results can I expect from LSA management?

Results depend on your market, budget, and competition, but our managed campaigns have seen a 28% increase in qualified leads and a 15% reduction in cost per lead within the first three months. We focus on lead quality, not vanity metrics. See our case study results.

Do I need a Google Guaranteed badge to run Local Services Ads?

Yes. The Google Guaranteed badge is required for home service businesses to appear in Local Services Ads. We handle the entire verification process for you, including documentation submission and background check coordination. Step-by-step guide to getting Google Guaranteed.

What's the difference between Google LSA and Google Ads?

Google Local Services Ads appear at the very top of search results (above traditional Google Ads) and you only pay per lead, not per click. Google Ads uses pay-per-click pricing and appears below LSA. Many contractors benefit from running both. Full comparison: LSA vs. Google Ads.
