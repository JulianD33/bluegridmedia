FAQs About Local Services Ads

Is my business eligible for Local Services Ads?

LSAs are available to a wide range of service-based businesses including contractors, home services, healthcare providers, legal professionals, and more. Eligibility varies by industry and location—we'll check and guide you through setup.

How much should I budget for Local Services Ads?

Start with $1,000–$1,500/month, customized to your market and goals. Pay only for verified leads to maximize efficiency.

What counts as a qualified lead?

A direct contact from your ad—call, message, or booking—from someone in your service area. Google handles spam filtering.

Can you guarantee leads?

No guarantees in marketing, but our optimization ensures you're visible to searching customers, with most clients seeing leads soon after launch.

How do I track results?

Access clear reports on leads, spend, and trends, plus Google's dashboard for call reviews and disputes.

How long does LSA setup take?

Typically 7–10 business days after submitting documents, varying by industry and region.

Do you handle leads and disputes?

Yes, we monitor quality, dispute invalids, and optimize to safeguard your budget and boost ROI.
