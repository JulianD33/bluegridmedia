How to Improve Your Appliance Repair LSA ROI

  • Enable every appliance type. Refrigerator, washer, dryer, dishwasher, oven, microwave — each is a separate job type. Enable them all to capture maximum search volume.
  • Offer same-day service. Appliance emergencies drive same-day demand. Advertising same-day availability increases booking rate significantly.
  • Charge a diagnostic fee. A $75–$100 diagnostic fee that applies toward repair improves your average job value and filters out low-intent callers.
  • Collect reviews after every repair. Appliance repair customers who had a fast, successful fix are highly motivated to leave positive reviews.

Why Appliance Repair Converts Well on LSA

Appliance repair searches are almost always transactional — the person searching has a broken appliance and needs it fixed. There's very little comparison shopping or research phase. This means booking rates on answered calls tend to be higher than in categories where homeowners are still deciding whether to proceed. A strong answer rate and a clear, confident booking process are the two biggest levers for appliance repair LSA performance.
