How to Improve Your Electrician LSA ROI

  • Enable high-value job types. Panel upgrades, EV charger installation, generator installation, and whole-home rewiring are high-ticket job types that significantly improve average job value. Make sure all are enabled in your LSA profile.
  • Highlight licensing and insurance prominently. Homeowners are more cautious about electrical work than almost any other trade. Your Google Guaranteed badge, license number, and insurance details should be complete and visible.
  • Answer calls quickly. Electrical emergencies — no power, tripped breakers that won't reset, burning smells — drive urgent calls. Speed-to-answer is critical for booking rate on these high-value leads.
  • Upsell panel inspections on every job. Any service call is an opportunity to inspect the panel and present an upgrade quote if the panel is aging or undersized. This is one of the highest-margin upsells in electrical work.
  • Collect reviews after every job. Electrical customers who had a safe, professional experience are motivated to leave reviews. A simple text follow-up requesting a review can dramatically improve your review velocity and LSA ranking.

EV Charger Installation: A Growing LSA Opportunity for Electricians

EV charger installation is one of the fastest-growing job types in residential electrical work. Homeowners searching for EV charger installation are high-intent, often have newer homes with modern panels, and are willing to pay premium prices for quality work. Average job values for Level 2 charger installation run $800–$2,500 depending on panel work required.

Make sure "EV charger installation" is enabled as a job type in your LSA profile and that your profile mentions this service explicitly. This is a high-value, lower-competition keyword that can significantly improve your average job value from LSA leads.
