How to Improve Your Pest Control LSA ROI

  • Convert one-time callers to service plans. Every initial call is an opportunity to enroll a customer in a quarterly or monthly plan. This is where your real ROI comes from.
  • Enable all pest types. Ants, rodents, termites, bed bugs, mosquitoes — each is a separate job type in LSA. Enable them all to maximize lead volume.
  • Answer calls immediately. Pest emergencies (rodents, bed bugs) drive urgent calls. Speed-to-answer is critical for booking rate.
  • Collect reviews after every service visit. Pest control customers who are happy with ongoing service are ideal review sources — they've seen your work multiple times.

Why Recurring Revenue Changes the Math for Pest Control LSA

A one-time general pest treatment might be worth $180. But that same customer on a quarterly plan at $90/visit for two years is worth $720 — four times the initial job value. When you calculate ROAS using only the first job, you're dramatically undervaluing what each LSA lead is actually worth to your business.

Use the "Recurring revenue" toggle in the calculator above to enter your average monthly plan value and typical retention period. The LTV-adjusted ROAS is the number you should be using to make budget decisions.
