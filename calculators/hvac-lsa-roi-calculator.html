How to Improve Your HVAC LSA ROI

  • Enable emergency and after-hours job types. AC failures in summer and furnace failures in winter are the highest-converting, highest-value calls you'll receive. Make sure these job types are enabled and you're available to answer.
  • Sell maintenance agreements on every service call. A $15–$25/month maintenance plan customer retained for two years is worth $360–$600 in recurring revenue on top of the initial job. Turn on the recurring revenue toggle in the calculator to see the impact.
  • Answer calls within the first ring. HVAC emergencies are urgent. Homeowners call the next result if you don't answer. An answering service that can dispatch technicians is essential during peak season.
  • Increase budget during peak seasons. Summer (June–August) and winter (December–February) drive the highest lead volume. Raise your budget cap before these windows to avoid missing leads due to budget limits.
  • Collect reviews after every job. HVAC customers who had their comfort restored quickly are highly motivated to leave positive reviews. A simple text follow-up requesting a review can dramatically improve your review velocity.

HVAC Seasonal Strategy for LSA

HVAC LSA performance is highly seasonal. Lead volume and CPL both shift significantly between peak and off-peak periods. During summer and winter peaks, demand surges and competition for ad placement increases — but so does booking rate, because every call is urgent.

In shoulder seasons (spring and fall), consider running tune-up and maintenance specials to keep lead volume steady and build your maintenance plan customer base. These customers generate recurring revenue through the off-season and are your best source of replacement job referrals when systems eventually fail.

Why HVAC Companies Win with LSA

HVAC sits at the intersection of emergency intent and high job value — a combination that makes LSA economics exceptionally favorable. When a homeowner's AC fails on a 95-degree day, they are not comparison shopping. They call the first trusted result they see. Google Local Services Ads with the Google Guaranteed badge is that result.

Unlike Google Search Ads where you pay per click regardless of whether the person calls, LSA charges only when a qualified lead contacts you directly. For HVAC companies with strong answer rates and booking processes, this pay-per-lead model consistently outperforms traditional PPC on a cost-per-booked-job basis.
