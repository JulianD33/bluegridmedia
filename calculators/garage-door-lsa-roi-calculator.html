How to Improve Your Garage Door LSA ROI

  • Answer every call, especially after hours. Garage door emergencies happen at night and on weekends. An answering service that can book appointments is essential.
  • Enable opener installation and new door job types. These are higher-ticket jobs that dramatically improve your average job value.
  • Upsell during service calls. A spring repair customer is a natural candidate for a new opener or full door upgrade. Train technicians to present options.
  • Collect reviews immediately after service. Garage door customers are often relieved and grateful — the moment right after a successful repair is the best time to ask.

Why Garage Door Companies Win with LSA

Garage door is one of the few home service categories where emergency intent is extremely high and the decision cycle is very short. Homeowners don't comparison shop for three days — they call the first trusted result. LSA's position above all other search results, combined with the Google Guaranteed badge, makes it the dominant channel for garage door lead generation in most markets.
