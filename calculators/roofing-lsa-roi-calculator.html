How to Improve Your Roofing LSA ROI

  • Enable repair and inspection job types. Roof repairs and inspections are high-volume, lower-ticket jobs that often lead to replacement quotes. Enable these job types to maximize lead volume, then upsell replacements during the inspection.
  • Respond to storm damage calls immediately. After major hail or wind events, roofing lead volume spikes dramatically. Having your LSA profile fully optimized and your team ready to respond quickly is essential for capturing storm season revenue.
  • Increase budget after major weather events. Temporarily raising your budget cap after storms ensures you don't miss leads due to budget limits during the highest-demand periods of the year.
  • Collect reviews after every completed job. Roofing customers who had a smooth, professional experience are motivated to leave reviews. A simple text follow-up requesting a review can dramatically improve your review velocity and LSA ranking.
  • Dispute invalid leads promptly. Roofing LSA can generate out-of-area and wrong-number calls. Dispute these within 30 days to get credits and keep your cost-per-lead metrics accurate.

Storm Season Strategy for Roofing LSA

Roofing LSA performance is highly event-driven. A single major hail storm can generate more leads in 48 hours than a typical month. The roofing companies that win storm season are the ones with fully optimized LSA profiles — verified, with strong reviews and all job types enabled — before the storm hits.

Consider setting a higher budget cap year-round so you're never limited when demand surges. The cost of a missed lead during storm season far exceeds the cost of a slightly higher monthly budget during slow periods.

Why Roofing Companies Win with LSA

Roofing is a high-trust, high-stakes purchase. Homeowners are spending $10,000–$20,000 and want to hire a company they can trust. The Google Guaranteed badge — which requires background checks, license verification, and insurance — provides exactly that trust signal at the moment of search. This is why roofing companies with strong LSA profiles consistently outperform competitors relying on traditional Google Ads or organic search alone.

The combination of top-of-page placement, the Google Guaranteed badge, visible star ratings, and pay-per-lead pricing makes LSA the highest-ROI advertising channel for most roofing companies in competitive markets.
