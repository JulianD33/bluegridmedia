How to Improve Your Landscaping LSA ROI

  • Lead with a seasonal offer. Spring cleanup, fall leaf removal, and aeration are high-search-volume services that convert well and lead to ongoing contracts.
  • Enable all service types. Lawn mowing, landscaping design, irrigation, fertilization, snow removal — each is a separate job type that expands your reach.
  • Convert one-time jobs to contracts. Every cleanup or one-time mow is an opportunity to pitch a weekly or biweekly service plan.
  • Collect reviews after seasonal projects. Customers who just had their yard transformed are highly motivated to leave positive reviews.

Seasonal Strategy for Landscaping LSA

Landscaping LSA performance peaks in spring (March–May) and again in fall (September–October). Increase your budget during these windows to capture the highest-intent leads of the year. In slower winter months, consider reducing spend or shifting focus to snow removal if you offer it.

Year-round lawn care contracts acquired during peak season continue generating revenue through the off-season, making spring LSA investment especially high-value.
